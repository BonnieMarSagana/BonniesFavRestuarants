The Best Restaurants on Saipan

(In Bonnie's Opinion)

Welcome to Saipan's Culinary Paradise! Discover the island's exquisite dining destinations (In Bonnie's Opinion) as we embark on a journey
through the flavors of Saipan. Whether you're a local foodie or a visitor seeking unforgettable dining experiences, let me be your compass
to the best restaurants Saipan has to offer. Get ready to savor the flavors, ambiance, and culture that make dining on this stunning island
a truly memorable experience!

Best Places To Dine:

  1. Ocean Star Restaurant
  2. Lucky Bills
  3. 153 Restaurant Cafe

Ocean Star Restaurant

Ocean Star Restaurant is the ultimate destination for a wide range of cuisines. They serve food from several cultures, such as Chinese, Filipino, and Western cuisine. In addition to their great food, they even offer a karaoke room where you and your friends and family can enjoy.

Restaurant Highlights

  • Wide Variety of Food
  • Updated Karaoke Room (Smart TV)
  • Good Portions of Food for the Price

Lucky Bills

Lucky Bills offers a variety of scrumptious Thai food on their menu. Additionally, they offer a large number of vegetarian options, breakfast items, burgers, sandwiches, seafood, steak, and even Mexican food. Along with their dine-in option, they also provide a lunch buffet with various Thai and American dishes for only $15.

Restaurant Highlights

  • Great Quality Food
  • Amazing Prices
  • Endless Options

153 Restaurant Cafe

153 Restaurant Cafe has some of the best Filipino and Local Food on Island. They have dishes ranging from local breakfast items, sandwiches, steak, noodles, and other food items with a great price! Furthermore, the location of this establishment is rather convenient for college students attending Northern Marianas College (NMC).

Restaurant Highlights

  • Convenient Location For College Students
  • Large Portions
  • Friendly Staff
